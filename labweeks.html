    Weapon of choice

    Considering we decided to go for AR we did our research regarding which software would be the best for this. After a few beginner guides on different options and a good discussion about Pro's and Con's we settled on Unity. Unity recently added in the AR option in the software itself rather than using external add-ons that enabled this within Unity as it did formerly. Next to that I was eager to make my own 3D Model of the castle, I am fairly familiar with SketchUp it gave a special touch to have our own castle. We also found it a great benefit that this enables us to use the model in any future Portfolio seeing we would have ownership over it.

    Tracing

    To get started on this we used Vuforia, Vuforia is an add-on that uses their site as database and turns images into trace-ables. It seeks keypoints in picture to trace and gives a star rating on how good traceable the object is. This also became out first hurdle because we initial wanted to make the puzzle less "puzzle-y" by plainly using icons.
